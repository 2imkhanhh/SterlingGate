Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 21, 2026

At Sterling Gate Capital LLC, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

This policy explains how we handle the data collected through our website and consulting services.

Information Collection and Use

We collect information such as your Name, Email Address, and Phone Number solely for the purpose of providing professional consulting and financial services. This information allows us to communicate with you effectively regarding your commercial projects or funding inquiries.

Mobile Information and SNS Privacy

We maintain strict confidentiality regarding your mobile data:

  • No Sharing for Marketing: No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
  • Exclusion of Opt-In Data: All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties under any circumstances.

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to ensure your data is handled securely. We do not sell, rent, or lease our client lists to third parties. Your information is accessed only by authorized personnel to facilitate your business requests.

Confidentiality

Sterling Gate Capital operates on the principles of discretion and integrity. All client information, communications, deal structures, and financial details are treated with the highest level of confidentiality. We do not disclose client information to any outside party without explicit consent.

Third-party Services

Our website may utilize trusted third-party tools to enhance your experience, including:

  • Google Analytics: Used to understand website traffic and improve user experience. Data collected is aggregated and anonymous.
  • Calendly: Used for scheduling private consultations. Calendly maintains its own privacy policy governing the use of your scheduling information.

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them independently.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your information at any time.
  • Opt out of any future communications from Sterling Gate Capital.

Your Consents

By providing your contact information, you consent to its use as outlined in this policy.

If you wish to opt out of any future communications or have your data removed from our systems, please contact us directly using the information below.

Changes To This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time.

Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our website following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
